• GENERAL

    1. What is QIX?

      QIX is a digital currency that you can earn by engaging with your daily habits and get rewarded with QIX or exchange from other loyalty programs. QIX digital currency can already be spent in more than 500.000 locations globally in combination with any other fiat currency​.

    2. Is QIX a crypto currency?​

      No, QIX is a cross border digital currency linked to regulated fiat currencies such as the Euro, British pound sterling, Australian or American dollars. Unlike a crypto currency is always 100% value of the liability secured by deposits which are held in higly reliable state bonds. 

    3. Is QIX secure? ​

      Unlike any other points currency in the world QIX holds a secure 100% cash reserve against the number of QIX in circulation. The balance will be held in the leading six global fiat currencies, at a top 50 leading European bank. A leading top 4 audit firm oversees the compliance of this depositing strategy. ​

    4. How will the value of my QIX be determined when I am outside my registered home country?​

      The value of QIX will be displayed in the mobile app based on real time currency calculation if QIX user is outside his home currency market. For example, if the actual rate of exchange is EUR 1.00 = AUD 1.6. a QIX account holder from Australia with a balance of 10 000 QIX, when arriving in a EURO zone market will see the value of those QIX displayed in the app as € 62.50 while a EURO zone registered QIX currency holder would see the value of 10 000 QIX as € 100.00
